Character Growth App

Character Lab, a nonprofit dedicated to developing character strengths in children, is developing an iOS app to help middle- and high school- students grow in 9 different key strength areas. This is done through personal self-assessments, assessments by their teachers, and by giving "Kudos" to one another to recognize when these strengths are used. In designing the app, one of the challenges I faced was to make it kid-friendly and fun enough for kids as young as 11 and mature enough for kids as old as 18.
